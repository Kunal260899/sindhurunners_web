About Us

Sindhu Runners is an established running community consisting of over 300 runners from all parts of Sindhudurg. The team members include highly qualified professionals such as doctors, pharmacists, engineers, retired army officers, government officers, chartered accountants, and other health enthusiasts, all coming together under the name "Sindhu Runners". We practice daily and share our thoughts and training tips with new members to help them grow in their potential. Our goal is to encourage participation in various running events in other cities and represent the Sindhudurg district. We are also spreading fitness awareness among people and children, and this is only possible with your valuable cooperation in organizing events.

Prasad Korgaonkar and Omkar Paradkar of the Sindhu Runners team represented the country and brought recognition to both the district and the nation at the world-famous Comrades Marathon 2024 Up Run held in South Africa. Their finishing times are as follows:
- Prasad Korgaonkar: Bill Rowan Medal (8 hours 51 minutes)
- Omkar Paradkar: Bronze Medal (10 hours 46 minutes)

Additionally, they have taken on the following roles:
1. Race Ambassador: Kokan Coastal Half Marathon 2024
2. Race Ambassador: Kudal Monsoon Half Marathon 2024
3. Pacer: Comrades Qualifier Pacer 4:49 at the Pune International Marathon
